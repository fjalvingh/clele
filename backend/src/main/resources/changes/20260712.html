Dark mode

Clele now has a dark theme — a low-contrast, muted palette (similar in spirit to the Darcula editor theme) rather than a stark black-and-white inversion. Switch it under My Account > Appearance: choose Light, Dark, or System (follows your OS setting and updates live if it changes). The choice is remembered on this device.

Edit/delete moved off the parts list

The parts search results no longer have per-row Edit/Delete buttons — click a part to open its detail page instead. The part detail page now has a Delete button next to Edit (for users with part-edit rights) so deleting a part still works, just from one place.
